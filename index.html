Why did we create PeopleFinder?

During the Hackathon “Build for Belarus”, we created PeopleFinder, as a solution for finding missing people. We make it possible for people who are in search for their missing relatives and friends to get more information about his or her last activity that was captured on a photo or video in the open data sources.

How does it work?

We created a Telegram bot where people could upload a picture of the missing person. With the help of face recognition technology, our system would tell whether there is a match between an uploaded photo and data from the open sources or not. If there is a match, it means that a missing person was pictured on a photo or video from the public sources. It would hint to a person, where his relative/friend noticed the last time. Also, we have a single-page website as a backup for people to upload pictures of missing people if Telegram is down or does not want to use it.
